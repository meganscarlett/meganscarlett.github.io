apbiology

Welcome to apbiology! This site is an amalgamation of my experience and learning in the AP Biology course throughout the 2017-2018 school year. Here you can find all of my notes with the related textbook chapters in the AP Campbell Biology Tenth Edition book, science stories that I find interesting, and pictures and stories about the labs I have completed as a part of my course this year. There is also a contact page should you wish to message me with any questions about AP Biology content. I hope students in the course can find this content helpful in revising and succeeding in their own AP Biology course.
